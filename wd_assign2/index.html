Welcome to Sweet Life!

Step into a world where every scoop is a celebration of flavor and every bite is a journey of delight.
Since 2005, Sweet Life has been crafting exquisite ice creams that tantalize the taste buds and warm the heart. From our humble beginnings to becoming a beloved local hotspot, we've remained committed to serving up smiles.
Join us as we invite you to experience the sweetest moments and create cherished memories at Sweet Life.

About Us

Established in 2005, Sweet Life has been a beloved destination for ice cream lovers in the heart of our community. Founded by two childhood friends with a passion for crafting exceptional frozen treats, Sweet Life began as a small, humble shop. Over the years, it has grown into a cherished local institution, known for its commitment to quality, creativity, and, of course, delicious ice cream.

Our Menu


Classic Vanilla

Our Classic Vanilla ice cream boasts the perfect balance of sweetness and richness, with the subtle aroma of Madagascar vanilla beans. It's a timeless indulgence that never fails to delight the senses.

Decadent Chocolate

Dive into a world of pure indulgence with our Decadent Chocolate ice cream, featuring a velvety-smooth texture and an intense cocoa flavor. Each spoonful is a luxurious treat for chocolate lovers.

StrawB. Bliss

Delight in the refreshing burst of flavor found in our Strawberry Bliss ice cream, made with the juiciest ripe strawberries. Its creamy consistency and fruity essence make it the perfect summertime indulgence.

Location

You can find Sweet Life nestled in the heart of downtown, conveniently located at 80 Duy Tan Street, Ha Noi. Our cozy shop offers a warm and welcoming atmosphere where you can relax and indulge in your favorite ice cream treats. Whether you're stopping by for a quick scoop or spending time with friends and family, Sweet Life is the perfect destination for sweet moments and unforgettable memories.
