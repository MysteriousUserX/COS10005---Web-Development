Embracing the Joystick: A Passion for Video Games

Written by: Nguyen Duc Tam

Today, I write this article to delve into the wonderful world of video games and share my undying passion for this immersive form of entertainment. As someone who has spent countless hours exploring virtual realms, battling formidable foes, and unraveling epic narratives, I can confidently say that gaming isn't just a pastime for me—it's a way of life. As such, I would like to introduce some of my favourite games that I am playing right now.

Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact stands as a groundbreaking open-world action role-playing game crafted by miHoYo. Set in the mesmerizing realm of Teyvat, players embark on an epic journey as the "Traveler," a character on a quest to reunite with their lost sibling and unravel the enigmatic mysteries of this fantastical world.

Honkai Impact 3rd

Honkai Impact 3rd transports players into a post-apocalyptic world teetering on the brink of annihilation. Developed by miHoYo, this 3D action role-playing mobile game thrusts players into the role of Valkyries, powerful warriors tasked with defending humanity against the relentless onslaught of the enigmatic Honkai.

Honkai: Star Rail

Honkai Star Rail, the latest installment in the acclaimed Honkai series by miHoYo, promises to redefine the role-playing genre with its innovative gameplay and captivating storyline. Set in the same universe as Honkai Impact 3rd, Honkai Star Rail introduces players to a band of extraordinary characters known as the "Starlit Astrologers," who possess the ability to manipulate time and space.

Conclusion: Long Live Gaming!

In conclusion, gaming isn't just a hobby—it's a vibrant and dynamic form of entertainment that has enriched my life in countless ways.

Thank you for joining me on this journey through the wonderful world of gaming. Until next time!
